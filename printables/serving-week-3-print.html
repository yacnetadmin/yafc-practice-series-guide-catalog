Week Three: Growing Up in Christ

"People who know that they are embedded in an eternal community are both freed and empowered to become who they were born to be." Parker J. Palmer, The Active Life

There is no mature, isolated Christian life. Serving one another is not optional to growth; it is one of the central means by which we are formed into Christ's likeness.

Discuss in Community

Read Ephesians 4:11-16 and discuss:

  • What does it mean to equip the saints for ministry in your context?
  • When did you experience the greatest growth in your spiritual journey?
  • What does it practically mean that we belong to one another?

Questions for Community

  1. How are you currently serving the body, and do you sense joy and growth in it?
  2. Is there a way you might serve this group more intentionally?
  3. How willing are you to rearrange your schedule to meet needs?

Individual Practice

Assess your current service posture and commit to either a new regular area of ministry or one intentional unplanned act of service this week.
