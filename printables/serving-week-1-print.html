Week One: How to be Great

"Whoever would be great must be your servant." Matthew 20:26

Jesus reframes greatness through service. The path to the top in the kingdom begins and ends at the bottom.

Discuss in Community

Read Matthew 20:20-28 and discuss:

  • Why do you think Jesus references "the cup that I am to drink" in this moment?
  • How have you measured spiritual health, and how does that compare to Jesus' metric?
  • Do you desire to be a "great Christian"? Why?

Questions for Community

  1. What are your initial thoughts stepping into this service practice?
  2. What emotion surfaces when you think about living more deeply into service?
  3. Have you had someone meet a practical need for you, or done that for others?

Individual Practice

This week, intentionally serve someone already in your relational circle. Choose a specific act you would not naturally do otherwise, and do it quietly. Reflect on barriers, emotions, and how you sensed Jesus in the process.
