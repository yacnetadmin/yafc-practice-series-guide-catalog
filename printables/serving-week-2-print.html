Week Two: Gifts and Grace

"Don't think you are better than you really are." Romans 12:3 (NLT)

Everything is gift: breath, time, ability, personality, and spiritual gifting. Recognizing this helps us serve with humility rather than ego.

Discuss in Community

Read Romans 12:1-13 and discuss:

  • How do verses 3-13 connect with verses 1-2?
  • How does your group currently recognize that all members can participate in the Spirit's work?
  • What changes could help your meetings better reflect that reality?

Practice in Community

Walk through gifting discernment together: listen, reflect, identify strengths, and discern a practical way each person can test their gifting through service.

Individual Practice

Use SHAPE as a guide: Spiritual gifts, Heart, Abilities/talents, Personality, and Experiences. Note overlaps and identify your next faithful step of service.
